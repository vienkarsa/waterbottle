About us

We want the best for them, just like we want the best for you. AquaPaw was created for the pets, who are famous for their carefree spirit and unconditional love. They can cheer us up when we are feeling down and give us a laugh when we need one. We wanted to do something for them too, that is how AquaPaw came to life. All pets are living for our sake, we are existing for their sake.
